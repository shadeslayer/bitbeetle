THE PROBLEM

Support is broken at the handoff

B2B SaaS companies lose hours — and customers — when support and engineering operate without shared context.

01 — FRAGMENTATION
Support teams drown in noise
Email, Slack, and Teams tickets arrive in separate tools. Nothing is unified. Context is lost between channels.
02 — ESCALATION
Engineers lose hours reconstructing bugs
Developers spend more time understanding what the customer experienced than actually fixing the root cause.
03 — KNOWLEDGE LOSS
Knowledge evaporates between tickets
Every resolved ticket is an opportunity to learn. Without a persistent knowledge layer, the same issues repeat.
